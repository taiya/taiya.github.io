Biography

I am a senior research scientist at Google Brain (Toronto), and an adjunct faculty at the University of Toronto. My research focuses on 3D Computer Vision (i.e. inverse graphics). I spent 2018 as a visiting faculty in Daydream Augmented Perception (Shahram Izadi) working on 4D data (capture, tracking, compression, modeling, simulation of geometry). Before joining Google, I was an assistant professor at the University of Victoria (2015-2017), where I held the "Industrial Research Chair in 3D Sensing". My alma mater includes EPFL (postdoc, Mark Pauly), SFU (PhD, Richard Zhang and Daniel Cohen-or, NSERC Alexander Graham Bell fellow), and Politecnico di Milano (gold medalist in graduate studies).
